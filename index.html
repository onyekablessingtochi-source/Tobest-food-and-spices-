About Us

Our food community is a group of people committed to promoting healthy eating, food sharing, and nutrition education. We believe food brings people together and strengthens society.

Our Mission

  • Promote balanced and nutritious diets
  • Support families facing food challenges
  • Educate on food hygiene and safety
  • Reduce food waste in our community

Our Activities

  1. Nutrition sensitization programs
  2. Community cooking demonstrations
  3. Food distribution outreach
  4. School and CDS awareness campaigns
